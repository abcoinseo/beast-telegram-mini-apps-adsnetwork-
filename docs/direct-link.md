# Adsbitvex Direct Link Ads 🚀

[Adsbitvex](https://adsbitvex.com) now supports **Direct Link Ads**, allowing instant monetization for publishers.

---

## How Direct Link Works

Your link format:
https://www.adsbitvex.com/?link=XXXXXX

> Replace `XXXXXX` with your unique advertiser link ID.

When someone visits this link:

- They are **instantly redirected** to the advertiser's page.  
- You earn **money for every click**.  

---

## Creating a Direct Link

1. Log in to your [Adsbitvex Publisher Dashboard](https://adsbitvex.com/?page=publisher).  
2. Go to **Direct Links** → Click **Create New Link**.  
3. Enter your **advertiser URL** and save.  
4. Copy the **generated link** and share anywhere.
